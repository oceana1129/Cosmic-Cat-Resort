About The Cosmic Cat Resort

Our Founders

The Imaginative Minds Who Started it All

Stella

Stella is a well know intergalactic traveler and photographer in the Obilikue Galaxy. She was approached by the Cosmic Cat Resort crew early into it's development, in 3021.

With her penchant for photography and knowledge of the uncharted Junper Belt, she would act as a great asset to their venture.So with camera and map in hand, Stella began to chart and capture the layout of the Junper Belt. Key in discovering the perfect location for their resort.

Stella now helps the crew bring in new travelers via her modeling and photography.

Cathy

A girl with a mission and a dream, Cathy set forth on an adventure that would change the lives of her and others in the Obilikue Galaxy.

Cathy was a previous caretaker and owner of a cat shelter located in the 25th quadrant. Tired and frustrated with the lack of attention the cats would get - she came up with an idea of the Cosmic Cat Resort.

Cathy now takes care of all of the cats in the resort. She also helps to maintain the books and accounting of the resort ensuring optimal efficiency.

Kiara

Kiara had been involved early on in the project of the Cosmic Cat Resort. Although she was no cat connesior herself, she was roommates with Cathy - which gave her just enough experience.

Cathy had imaginative ideas for the resort, but she didn't have the ability to put it all together. That's where Kiara came in. With her charming tongue and eye for engineering - Kiara was able to get the resources they needed to build the resort together.

Kiara now acts as the spokesperson and general engineer of the resort.
